How your health score works

This score is designed as a wellbeing progress tool and not a medical assessment.

  • Daily score (max 10) rates today's food, activity, sleep, alcohol, mood, and a balance bonus.
  • 7-day score is the average of your last 7 daily scores.
  • Overall score is weighted: 0.5 x last 30 days, 0.3 x days 31-90, and 0.2 x older days (renormalised to available periods).
  • Food uses combined fruit and veg servings.
  • Daily activity combines step banding, daily exercise minutes, and a combo bonus.
  • Weekly exercise and weekly alcohol are supporting 7-day metrics and are not separate score modes.
  • Mood contributes directly and uses a neutral fallback when mood is not logged.
